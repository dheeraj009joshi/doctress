The journey to becoming a doctor is undoubtedly a laborious one
Long hours, high stress, and the constant demands of the profession can leave you feeling drained, overwhelmed, and disconnected from your true self. Unfortunately, for many women, the pressures of marriage can further limit their time and compromise their sense of self. It's natural to feel like we're losing ourselves in the routine of it all, with seemingly no way out. But what if we told you that there is a way to reclaim your passion, rediscover your purpose, and find balance in your life and work? Join us on a journey to break free from the grind, heal from burnout, and rediscover the joy and fulfillment of being a female doctor - by celebrating yourself.

Come, lets get liberated

Introducing Doctress

Doctress is a one-of-its-kind community that prioritizes the interests of women doctors. Our ecosystem is designed to promote growth and mental well-being, empowering female doctors to express themselves without fear of bias or judgement. We believe that women doctors deserve a platform to feel privileged, express their opinions and lead the way in their fields. We understand that women doctors struggle to balance their personal and professional lives. That's why we provide support at various levels, allowing you to rediscover the interests that make you who you are. Come and be a part of this like-minded community, where your voice is heard and your personality is celebrated. This is a safe space to let loose, be yourself and feel contented.
What are you waiting for??

Express

Looking to unwind and explore your creative side outside of medicine? At Doctress, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities to help you be your authentic self. Our community provides a safe and welcoming space where you can relax and let your guard down without the pressure of being treated like a celebrity. Join us for our weekend events, where you can either participate or simply enjoy the show as a member of the audience. From dance, culinary arts, to singing, fashion, and art exhibitions, there's something for everyone.

Network

Be a part of our community where women doctors can take charge. We provide a platform where you can network with like-minded women professionals across specialties, build rapport and strengthen your portfolio. Our community is exclusive where you can feel free to share your thoughts as well as ideas and have a say in the events that are organised. Unlike the usual conferences in the medical field, which might be male-centric, we provide opportunities for women to be the forefront and lead the events and panels.

Find your tribe and empower yourself with Doctress!

Grow

Doctress is committed to provide support to female doctors at all levels, including those in peripheries and small towns. That's why we offer mental health sessions, workshops and one-on-one counseling. Our sessions are available both offline and online, ensuring flexibility and accessibility regardless of their location. We also understand that support from other female doctors can be very valuable, which is why we have created a community that enables women doctors to support one another.
